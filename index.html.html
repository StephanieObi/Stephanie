About

"Good design doesn't announce itself. It makes the right thing feel obvious."
Based in Lagos, Nigeria
Experience 4+ years
Focus SaaS · AI · Mobile
Background Design + Engineering

I started designing at 15. I've been interrogating conventions ever since.

My work lives at the intersection of systems thinking and editorial instinct — I'm drawn to the kinds of problems where the information itself is complex and the designer's job is to make it feel inevitable. Fraud dashboards. AI marketplaces. Cross-border payment flows. Mobile apps that carry real weight. The work that most designers find intimidating is usually the work I find most interesting.

I started designing at 15. I came up through frontend engineering first, which means I design with production constraints in mind — not despite them. That foundation shapes everything: how I think about components, how I hand off, how I build.
